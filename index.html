About Us

Jamia Mahmoodiyya is an Islamic Based institution founded by Usthad Nasrudheen Darimi.

It consists of various Educational institutions including Da'wa College, Hifz College, Women's College, CBSE School, Madrasa and Masajid.

The institution has been serving the community for about forty years. We request you to help our institutions as much as you can and let's together develop tomorrow's leaders.
