About Us

Jamia Mahmoodiyya is an Islamic Based institution founded by Usthad Nasrudheen Darimi.

It consists of various Educational institutions including Da'wa College, Hifz College, Women's College, CBSE School, Madrasa and Masajid.

The institution has been serving the community for about forty years. We request you to help our institutions as much as you can and let's together develop tomorrow's leaders.

Our Journey

Over four decades of excellence in education and spirituality.

1986

Foundation

Jamia Mahmoodiyya was founded by Usthad Nasrudheen Darimi with a vision for holistic Islamic education.

1995

Expansion

Opening of the first dedicated hifz college campus to accommodate growing numbers of students.

2010

English School

Establishment of Mahmoodiyya English School (CBSE) to integrate secular and spiritual excellence.

2024

Modernization

Launch of Edu Village and Smart Seed Premium Preschool, redefining modern Islamic learning environments.
