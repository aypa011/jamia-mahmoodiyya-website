Upcoming & Past Events

Stay updated with the latest happenings at Jamia Mahmoodiyya.

October 15, 2025

Maharjanul Mahabba

Join us for the grand annual gathering, Maharjanul Mahabba. A day filled with inspiring talks, cultural performances, and spiritual reflections.

Maharjanul Mahabba is Jamia Mahmoodiyya's flagship annual gathering, bringing together thousands of students, scholars, alumni, and community members in a celebration of knowledge and love for the Prophet ﷺ.

  • Grand Moulid & Qaseedah recitations
  • Keynote addresses by senior scholars
  • Student cultural and oratory performances
  • Prize distribution & academic recognition
  • Community fellowship and evening banquet

  Jamia Mahmoodiyya Campus, Perinjanam  |    9:00 AM – 9:00 PM

September 5, 2025

Meelad Rali

A beautiful procession organized by the students and community members to celebrate Meelad-un-Nabi with great zeal and devotion.

Meelad Rali is one of the most cherished annual traditions at Jamia Mahmoodiyya, where students, teachers, and the wider community march together in joyful procession celebrating the birth of the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ.

  • Community procession through Perinjanam town
  • Moulid & Salawaat recitations throughout the march
  • Banner and flag displays by each department
  • Special Nasheeds by the Jamia choir
  • Concluding Dua and communal meal

  Starts at Jamia Gate, Perinjanam  |    7:00 AM – 12:00 PM

August 20, 2025

Educational Seminar

An interactive seminar focusing on modern educational challenges and the integration of Islamic values in contemporary studies.

The Annual Educational Seminar brings together educators, scholars, and students to explore how Islamic principles can guide and enrich modern academic pursuits. Sessions are conducted in Arabic, Malayalam, and English.

  • Panel discussions with visiting scholars
  • Student paper presentations & debates
  • Workshop: Digital tools in Islamic education
  • Session: Integrating Tarbiyah in curriculum
  • Certificate distribution for participants

  Jamia Main Hall, Perinjanam  |    10:00 AM – 5:00 PM
